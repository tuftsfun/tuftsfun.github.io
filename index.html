What is FUN in the Afternoon?

FUN in the Afternoon is the functional programming interest group at Tufts CS. The new FUN is mainly for undergraduates, although graduate students (and faculty) are also welcome. Here we will discuss fun and interesting uses of and ideas in functional programming. Whether you're still in COMP 105 (and love it), or you have programmed your digital hourglass in functional style, you're more than welcome to join us!
