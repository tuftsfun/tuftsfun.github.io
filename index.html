What is FUN in the Afternoon?

FUN in the Afternoon is the functional programming interest group at Tufts CS. FUN is mainly for undergraduates, although graduate students are also welcome. Here we will discuss fun and interesting uses of and concepts related to functional programming. If you enjoyed COMP 105 and want to learn more about FP, drop by our meetings!
