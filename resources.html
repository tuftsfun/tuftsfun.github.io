Resources for Tufts functional programmers


Languages to learn

  • OCaml: essentially another dialect of ML, very similar to SML taught in COMP 105
  • Haskell: a pure functional language with many modern features; hallmark of FP research
  • Racket: an outgrowth of Scheme, perfect for building DSLs
  • Erlang: functional programming meets distributed systems
  • Scala: functional programming meets OOP on the JVM!

Language learning resources

  • Google
  • Real World series: Real World OCaml and Real World Haskell
  • Learn You some Haskell (and its Erlang adaptation)
  • Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs (the marvelous SICP book)
  • The Little Schemer and The Reasoned Schemer
  • never a bad idea to go back to Norman's book sometimes!
